## Method lessons (saved to user memory)

**Don't trust paper note as ground truth**:
- v5.0 patch relied on paper notes in `paper-notes/` (54 markdown files)
- When A worker actually queried arxiv abstracts and project pages,
  it found significant discrepancies with the paper notes

**Always cross-verify A with B**:
- A worker accuracy is ~85% in 17 fact sample
- Without B cross-verify, the 2 venue errors (tu2025speede3dgs, zhang2024mega)
  would have been committed as wrong facts

**Subagent max_iterations is the bottleneck**:
- 70 subagent runs in 6 hours = 10.5 fact/hour
- 326 pending × 1/10.5 = 31 hours to complete (impossible overnight)
- Solution: skip low-risk fact types (citekey/year/venue after spot-check)
  and focus on numeric facts (the high-impact errors live in numbers)

**Web search returns Chinese blogs first** for 3DGS topics:
- CSDN / Sohu / 163 tech re-posts dominate
- For 4DGS-1K / L2D2-GS / 4DGRT arxiv PDF needed direct arxiv fetch
  (or 2nd-pass worker); 1st-pass often inconclusive

**Leaked Adreno 830 specs are unverifiable**:
- 2.5 W / 4.5 W / 1.8 W power, 30 sec thermal throttling
- All marked [leaked] in v5.0; not patchable
- A worker cannot refute or confirm without Qualcomm datasheet access
